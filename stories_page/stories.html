Why Choose Us

At Myitra, we are dedicated to providing eco-friendly hygiene products that promote sustainability and well-being. Our carefully curated selection includes biodegradable soaps, reusable sanitary pads, and bamboo toothbrushes. We believe in making a positive impact on the environment without compromising on quality. With Myitra, you can make a difference with every purchase, contributing to a greener future for all. Join us in our mission towards a cleaner planet.

Who are We

MYITRA Social Enterprise is dedicated to empowering B40 communities by addressing challenges in hygiene, education, and employment. Through sustainable, community-driven initiatives like the Kotak Cantik MYITRA strives to improve living standards while promoting dignity, health, and opportunity for all.

Our Impact

Raise Awareness

Period poverty is not only about products — it is also about knowledge and breaking taboos. We actively run campaigns, workshops, and discussions to raise awareness about menstrual health. By bringing these conversations into schools, universities, and local communities, we aim to educate, reduce stigma, and inspire positive change in society

Encourage Community Engagement

Our work encourages people to come together for a common cause. Volunteers, donors, and partners join us in creating an ecosystem of support that amplifies our mission. This collective effort strengthens the community spirit and gives individuals an opportunity to directly contribute towards eliminating period poverty.

Supporting B40 Communities

At Myitra, we are dedicated to addressing the struggles of the B40 community in Malaysia, especially women who face barriers in accessing basic hygiene products. Through our Kotak Cantik boxes, we provide affordable and essential care items that reduce the financial burden on families and ensure that women can live with dignity and confidence.

Driving Long-Term Impact

The impact of our work goes beyond immediate relief. By ensuring girls can stay in school, women can participate fully in society, and families can manage essential needs at lower costs, we are shaping a sustainable future. Moving forward, we aim to expand our reach, introduce eco-friendly hygiene solutions, and build partnerships that will multiply our positive impact across Malaysia.
